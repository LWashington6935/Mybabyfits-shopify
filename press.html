Press FAQ

How do I request an interview?
Email press@babyfits.example with your outlet, topic, and deadline. We reply within 1–2 business days.
May I use your photography?
Yes—any images in the Press Kit may be used for editorial coverage with credit “BabyFits”.
Are product samples available?
We maintain a limited sample closet for accredited media & select creators. Include sizing & purpose in your request.
Do you announce funding rounds?
We selectively publicize financing events once strategic milestones are achieved; inquire for embargoed details.
